Why I Love Nickelodeon

An animated video for a company I truly adore

Next

Project Details

  • Team: Tamanh Nguyen (and her 3 siblings, who literally drew one picture each)
  • Time: 2 Weeks
  • Tools Used: Audacity, iMovie, iPad Drawing App (the exact name escapes me)

Overview

Why I Love Nickelodeon was a short, animated video that I created in the hopes of being able to intern for their animation studio in Burbank, CA. Their application simply asked for a cover letter and resume, but I knew I had to do something special to stand out from other talented applicants--especially since I’d be up against film/media/tv and animation majors.

Process

I like to think of this project as a direct application of my UX knowledge. HR was my frustrated user, sifting through hundreds of applications a day. To make it easier on them, I sought to create a holistically engaging application.

From my research into their company culture, I knew that they valued creativity and passion for your craft. They value genuineness, and heart. They’re the type of company that truly cares about their employees, and wants to create nothing but the best content to make kids (and their parents) smile.

With this information, I knew that I needed to standout by showcasing my personality, and why I chose to do the things I do. I humored the idea of writing a song for them, but ultimately decided to create the video you see below. One script, a storyboard, 40 images, and an audio recording later, this video showcases who I am as a person, and combines the crafts and mediums I love most.

Lessons Learned

  • Go big or go home.
  • Don’t count on your baby sister to help you draw images for a video. She’ll leave you to play with kittens.
